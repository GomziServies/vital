Elaichi (cardamom) flavored peanut butter offers a unique and delicious twist on the classic spread.

It's a blend of creamy, roasted peanuts infused with the aromatic, slightly sweet, and spicy flavor of cardamom.

Directions of Use:

Perfect for spreading on bread, pancakes, or waffles.

Add to smoothies or protein shakes for a Elaichi twist.

Use as a dip for fruits like apple slices or bananas.

Enjoy straight from the jar as a sweet, energizing snack.

Versatile Use:

Elaichi peanut butter can be used in various ways, including sandwiches, on toast, or as a snack on its own.
